Florent Charpin is a recreational scuba diver and underwater photographer based in New York City. He has been diving since 2002 and has visited many tropical reefs around the world, including the Caribbean, the Pacific, Hawaii, the Eastern Pacific and French Polynesia. He is passionate about marine life and enjoys sharing his knowledge and photos with other divers and nature enthusiasts. He created this website as a personal project to document his diving experiences and to provide a comprehensive guide to the tropical reef species he has encountered.
Thank you for visiting and I hope you find this website useful and enjoyable.
